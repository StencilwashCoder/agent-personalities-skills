## Phase 2: First Customer Acquisition (Week 2-4)

### 2.1 Target Profile

**Ideal First Customer:**
- Seed/Series A dev tools company
- 5-20 employees
- Has a blog but it's neglected/outdated
- Technical founder who hates writing
- Budget: $1,000-2,000/month for content

### 2.2 Outreach Channels (Ranked by Priority)

#### Channel 1: Twitter/X DM Campaign
**Why**: Direct access to technical founders
**Volume**: 20-30 DMs/day
**Message Template**:

```
Hey [Name], saw your work on [specific project]. 

Quick question: Are you handling content marketing in-house 
or have you found a technical writer who actually understands 
[their tech stack]?

We've been building Stencilwash to solve exactly that - 
developers who write technical content for developer tools.

Worth a 10-min chat?
```

**Target List**:
- Dev tool founders with 1K-10K followers
- Recently funded startups (Crunchbase alerts)
- YC companies in current batch + 2 previous batches

#### Channel 2: Indie Hackers / Hacker News
**Tactics**:
- "How we're building a $20K MRR content agency" - transparency post
- Comment thoughtfully on relevant threads
- Offer free content audit in comments

#### Channel 3: LinkedIn Outreach
**Target**: DevRel managers, marketing leads at Series A+ startups
**Message**:

```
Hi [Name],

Noticed [Company] is investing heavily in developer marketing. 
Question: How do you ensure technical accuracy in your content?

We started Stencilwash specifically because we kept seeing 
"explainers" written by non-technical writers that missed 
the nuances developers care about.

Worth exploring if we could help with [specific content gap 
you noticed on their blog]?
```

#### Channel 4: Partner Network
**Approach**:
- Reach out to dev tools with complementary services
- Offer referral fee: 20% first month
- Target: Design agencies, dev shops, SEO consultants

### 2.3 The "Land" Offer

**First Customer Special**:
- First month: 50% off (Growth plan at $749 instead of $1,499)
- Guarantee: First article free if not satisfied
- Goal: Case study + testimonial

## Phase 4: Sales Process

### 4.1 Discovery Call Script (15 min)

1. **Context** (2 min)
   - "What does your current content workflow look like?"
   - "Who's writing your technical content now?"

2. **Pain** (5 min)
   - "What's the biggest frustration with your current approach?"
   - "How much time is [founder] spending on content vs product?"

3. **Vision** (5 min)
   - "If content wasn't a bottleneck, what would you publish?"
   - "What would success look like in 6 months?"

4. **Close** (3 min)
   - "Based on what you said, sounds like the Growth plan would fit."
   - "We can start with a pilot article if you want to test us out."

### 4.2 Objection Handling

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "Too expensive" | "What does a senior engineer's time cost? If you're spending 10 hrs/month on content, that's $3K+ in opportunity cost." |
| "We'll hire in-house" | "Great plan for later. We're the bridge - get content flowing now while you search for the perfect hire." |
| "We've tried writers before" | "So have we - that's why we started this. Our writers are developers first. Try one article, if it sucks, don't pay." |
| "Not a priority right now" | "Fair. Can I send you our 'Technical Content Scorecard' to see what you're missing? Takes 2 minutes." |
