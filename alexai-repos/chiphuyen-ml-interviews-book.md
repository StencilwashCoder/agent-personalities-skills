# chiphuyen/ml-interviews-book

## What It Does
Comprehensive machine learning interview preparation book by Chip Huyen. Covers 200+ knowledge questions across ML concepts, systems design, and practical implementation. Includes difficulty ratings and detailed explanations.

## Key Features
- **200+ Questions**: Covering fundamentals to advanced topics
- **Difficulty Ratings**: Know what to expect for each role level
- **Systems Design**: ML-specific architecture questions
- **Real Experience**: Based on interviews at Google, NVIDIA, Netflix
- **Free Online**: Full book available at huyenchip.com

## Productivity Impact
- Reference for hiring ML engineers
- Identify knowledge gaps in current team
- Standardize interview processes
- Stay current on ML interview trends

## Relevance: LOW-MEDIUM

Useful if/when Eric needs to hire ML engineers. Good reference for understanding what skills are valued in the industry.
